Dissertation Paper:

Project information

  • Category: Web Development - Dissertation
  • Project date: 05 September, 2022
  • Project URL: https://github.com/esiougkari/dissertation

Description

My dissertation was based on Digital Badges in Education. I created a system that provides a more customized look at each student's profile, enhancing future interviews and strengthening their job applications. The general idea of this project is a system that will make students feel recognizable and understand that their hard effort and achievements are rewarded with a tool that can also support them in their professional lives. This concept will motivate them to achieve more, start new activities, and aim for higher grades. The system is an ongoing project that future enhancements can complete.
